Rhytmical massage therapy (RMT)

...is part of the Anthroposophical Medical System. RMT is a light touch, warm, nurturing, enveloping massage therapy that enlivens and balances the body, calling forth the clients own healing forces. Specific massage movements of line, circle and lemniscate work with the relationship between the body, soul and spirit. The massage works with the life supporting forces of warmth, rhythm and breathing, as well as the interplay of levity and gravity, center and periphery.

Specific Indications for RMT
as defined by the RMTA

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Muscular tension, strain and injury
  • Back pain
  • Allergies
  • Immune ans auto immune disorders
  • Cardiovascular disorders
  • Respiratory Conditions
  • Digestive disorders
  • Eating disorders
  • Degenerative disorders
